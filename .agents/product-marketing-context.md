# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-04-01*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Legacy codebase modernization in 30 days with AI-powered workflows.
**What it does:** Sprint Zero audits, modernizes, and restructures legacy codebases so they're AI-ready — then hands off full ownership with a team workshop, custom AI skills package, and 30-day support window. The founder (Marcel, 15 years building software) works inside the codebase alongside coordinated AI systems, making architecture decisions and running quality control.
**Product category:** Codebase modernization / legacy code migration / technical debt remediation
**Product type:** Done-for-you service (fixed-scope engagement)
**Business model:** Fixed-price engagement starting at $35K, scoped after a free audit call. No hourly billing, no change orders. Capacity-capped at 2 engagements per month.

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Software companies and SaaS businesses doing $1M–$50M ARR with codebases 3+ years old
**Decision-makers:** Founders, CEOs, CTOs — people who can approve a $35K investment without a procurement committee
**Primary use case:** Codebase is holding the team back — no velocity, falling behind goals, can't leverage AI tools, can't pass compliance reviews, can't attract good engineers
**Jobs to be done:**
- Get the codebase into a state where AI dev tools actually work (unlock 10x developer velocity)
- Stop bleeding money on an underperforming outsourced team or an internal team fighting the architecture
- Pass an upcoming compliance review, security audit, or vendor assessment
- Make the codebase attractive to senior engineering candidates
- Increase company valuation ahead of a potential acquisition
**Use cases:**
- SaaS founder paying $8K–$15K/month to an outsourced team with nothing shippable to show for it
- Small internal team (1–8 devs) stuck maintaining instead of building — every PR takes 5x longer than it should
- Company approaching SOC 2, security audit, or big-customer vendor questionnaire with 100+ vulnerable dependencies
- Founder preparing for a conference, funding round, or product launch who needs the tech ready fast
- Company considering selling in 3–5 years and needs clean code for technical due diligence

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Legacy codebases with no tests, outdated architecture, and technical debt that kills velocity. Teams are stuck, behind on goals, and burning money maintaining instead of building.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Outsourced dev shops pick frameworks they know (not what's right), can't do responsive design or phased releases, bill hourly with no accountability, and keep the knowledge
- Big consultancies cost $150K–$300K, take 3–6 months, and don't upskill the team
- DIY with AI tools fails because AI needs clean architecture to generate usable code — legacy codebases speak a dialect these tools don't understand
- Hiring senior engineers fails because good candidates clone the repo, see the mess, and ghost
**What it costs them:**
- $10K–$25K/month in wasted dev spend (outsourced team billing with uncertain output, or internal team running at 60% efficiency)
- $120K–$150K+ burned over 6–12 months with the codebase actually getting worse
- 60% senior candidate rejection rate after seeing the repo
- $0 ROI on AI tools
- Failed compliance reviews, lost deals, existential business risk
**Emotional tension:** Frustration at paying and seeing nothing. Doubt about whether the team is the problem or the code is. Fear of the compliance conversation they know is coming. Helplessness — stuck in a situation they can't evaluate or fix themselves.
